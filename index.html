A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME...

I currently live in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada.

I have a BFA with distinction from the University of Victoria with a combined degree in Visual Arts and Computer Science. I am looking forward to expanding my knowledge in the fields of graphic design and software development and am interested in the integration of art with technology.

My main passion and focus is creativity. I have a wide range of artistic interests from painting, drawing and sculpture, to sewing, knitting and digital design. I enjoy finding inspiration from my travels and surroundings, and working in different mediums to create new pieces.
